## About Us

The North Harbour Sacred Heart Corporation is a federally-registered charitable organisation. Founded in 2004 (?), it is responsible and accountable for the purchase, enhancement, maintenance and operation of both the church and cemetery in North Harbour, St. Mary's Bay.

Our organization was founded following a Supreme Court of Appeal decision that found the Roman Catholic Episcopal Corporation of St. John's (the "Archdiocese") legally and vicariously liable for decades of sexual and physical abuse committed by the Irish Christian Brothers at Mount Cashel Orphanage. Following these liability rulings, the Archdiocese entered creditor protection, selling dozens of properties to fund over $100 million in victim compensation.

Many of its properties, including churches and parish halls in small communities, were ordered to be sold, although many were funded and built, not by the Archdiocese, but by the devout women and men in these communities. Our church was one of them.

A small group of current and former residents of the North Harbour decided to purchase the church. They generously donated sufficient funds to both incorporate our organisation, North Harbour Sacred Heart Church Corporation, and purchase the church. Several months later, negotiations with the Archdiocese resulted in the transfer of the cemetery to our organization.

Our Articles of Incorporation, By-Laws and Annual Financial Statements can be found under [Documents].
