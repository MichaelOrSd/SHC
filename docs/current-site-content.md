## Welcome to Our Church
*Written by Ida Linehan*

"In the early 1950s, Sacred Heart Church was erected by local carpenters as a community's gift to their faith, their deceased, their coming togetherness, and their future."

The narrative describes how the church holds the remains of multiple generations of family members. North Harbour (formerly Mullockville) has experienced tragedy — the Power brothers drowned in St. John's Narrows in the late 1800s.

The community's historical resilience comes through "rowing to the fishing grounds to eke out a living, turning the rocky soil to grow vegetables and raise animals." This hardship developed "perseverance, grit, and resilience" and "a supportive and generous spirit" passed through generations.

The church and community centre are "the heart of our community" — "everyone is welcome."
