# Site Design Prompts — Sacred Heart Church (nhshc.ca)

Use these prompts one at a time with Sonnet to generate 3 mockup concepts to present to Terry Power.

---

## Prompt 1: "Classic Heritage" — Traditional & Reverent

```
Design a full-page website mockup for Sacred Heart Church in North Harbour, St. Mary's Bay, Newfoundland. This is a small rural parish church established in the early 1950s. The design should feel traditional, reverent, and rooted in heritage.

BRAND:
- Primary color: deep red #8B0000
- Typography: serif fonts (Georgia or similar) for headings, clean sans-serif for body
- Tone: dignified, warm, historically grounded — reflects the "North Harbour gene of kindness, welcoming, embracing, and care"

PAGE LAYOUT — Single scrolling landing page with these sections:

1. HERO: Church name "Sacred Heart Church" with tagline referencing North Harbour, St. Mary's Bay, NL. Simple, elegant header with navigation links. No photo available yet — use a textured deep red background or subtle pattern.

2. OUR STORY: The history of the church — how the community came together in the early 1950s to purchase and establish it. Include a subsection about the community and its members. This is the heart of the site.

3. BOARD OF DIRECTORS: List of current leadership — President: Terry Power, Chairperson: Maryann Butt, Treasurer: Mary McGrath, Secretary: Lorraine Power. Text-only, no photos. Clean card or list layout.

4. CEMETERY: Section about the cemetery restoration work the community is doing. Include a visible link out to the gravesites database (external).

5. DONATE: Prominent donation section. E-Transfer to nhChurch@nhshc.ca, mail to North Harbour, NL A0B 2V0. Should feel inviting, not pushy.

6. NEWSLETTER SIGNUP: Simple email signup area for members to receive parish updates.

7. FOOTER: Contact info (nhshcinfo@gmail.com, mailing address), links to documents section (Articles of Incorporation, Financial Statements, Bylaws), and a small "Posts/Updates" link for future news.

STYLE DIRECTION: Think classic Catholic parish — dark reds, golds or cream accents, structured layout with clear hierarchy. Minimal, not cluttered. Could be a single-page site or very few pages. This should feel like it could be built for $800-1200 as a basic static site.
```

## Prompt 2: "Warm Community" — Welcoming & People-Focused

```
Design a full-page website mockup for Sacred Heart Church in North Harbour, St. Mary's Bay, Newfoundland. This is a small rural parish established in the early 1950s. The design should feel warm, welcoming, and community-centered — like an open door.

BRAND:
- Primary color: deep red #8B0000, paired with warm cream/ivory (#FFF8F0 or similar) and soft gold accents
- Typography: friendly serif for headings, warm readable sans-serif for body
- Tone: neighborly, inclusive, community-first — "the North Harbour gene of kindness, welcoming, embracing, and care"

PAGE LAYOUT — Single scrolling landing page with these sections:

1. HERO: Welcoming header — "Sacred Heart Church, North Harbour" with a warm greeting message. Navigation as a simple top bar or hamburger menu. No photos available yet — use a warm gradient, soft texture, or illustrated accent in deep red and cream.

2. OUR COMMUNITY: Lead with the people and the place. The story of North Harbour and how the community came together in the 1950s to purchase and build their church. Emphasize the members, the volunteer spirit, the care.

3. LEADERSHIP: Board of Directors presented in a warm, approachable way — President: Terry Power, Chairperson: Maryann Butt, Treasurer: Mary McGrath, Secretary: Lorraine Power. Text-only cards, no photos. Maybe a short note about transparency and volunteer governance.

4. CEMETERY RESTORATION: Tell the story of the community restoring the cemetery by hand. Terry Power has done much of the labour himself. Link to Shawn Power's gravesites database as an additional resource.

5. SUPPORT US / DONATE: Friendly, community-tone donation section. E-Transfer to nhChurch@nhshc.ca, mail donations to North Harbour, NL A0B 2V0. Frame it as supporting the community, not just the church.

6. STAY CONNECTED: Newsletter signup — "Join our parish family" or similar. Simple email input with a warm call to action.

7. FOOTER: Contact (nhshcinfo@gmail.com, address), document links (Articles of Incorporation, Financial Statements, Bylaws), and a "News & Updates" link for future posts section.

STYLE DIRECTION: Warm and inviting, like a community hall notice board done beautifully. Rounded corners, soft shadows, generous whitespace. Approachable rather than formal. Should feel achievable as a $800-1200 static site build, but with room to grow into a multi-page site later.
```

## Prompt 3: "Modern & Clean" — Contemporary with Heritage Roots

```
Design a full-page website mockup for Sacred Heart Church in North Harbour, St. Mary's Bay, Newfoundland. This is a small rural parish established in the early 1950s. The design should feel modern and clean while honoring the church's deep heritage — contemporary presentation, traditional soul.

BRAND:
- Primary color: deep red #8B0000 used as a bold accent, with a clean white/light gray base
- Typography: modern serif or slab-serif for headings (strong, confident), clean sans-serif for body
- Tone: confident, forward-looking, but respectful of roots — a small community with big heart

PAGE LAYOUT — Single scrolling landing page with these sections:

1. HERO: Bold, minimal header. "Sacred Heart Church" large and confident, "North Harbour, St. Mary's Bay, Newfoundland" as subtitle. Deep red accent bar or stripe. Clean navigation. No photos yet — use bold typography and color blocking as the visual.

2. OUR HISTORY: Clean storytelling section — the founding in the 1950s, how the community came together to purchase the church, and what it means to North Harbour today. Modern layout with clear sections for church history and community story.

3. WHO WE ARE: Board of Directors in a clean grid or modern list — President: Terry Power, Chairperson: Maryann Butt, Treasurer: Mary McGrath, Secretary: Lorraine Power. No photos, just names and roles. Possibly with a brief transparency statement.

4. CEMETERY PROJECT: The restoration work presented as a modern "project" or "initiative" section. Highlight the hands-on community effort. Clean link out to Shawn Power's gravesites database.

5. DONATE: Clear, modern donation call-to-action. E-Transfer to nhChurch@nhshc.ca, mail to North Harbour, NL A0B 2V0. Consider a button-style layout. Direct and easy to act on.

6. NEWSLETTER: Clean email signup. Minimal — one input, one button. "Get parish updates" or similar.

7. FOOTER: Streamlined — contact info (nhshcinfo@gmail.com, address), document links (Articles of Incorporation, Financial Statements, Bylaws), future posts/updates section link.

STYLE DIRECTION: Think modern nonprofit or small-town museum site. Lots of whitespace, bold typography, the deep red as a powerful accent rather than a background. Flat design, no clutter. This is the "basic now, expandable later" option — starts as a single page at $800-1200 but the design system could scale to a full multi-page site with posts, events, and more in a future phase.
```

---

## Notes for Presenting to Terry

- All three designs cover the same content and functionality
- Differences are in tone and visual style — let Terry react to the feeling
- Whichever direction he picks, Phase 1 build is the same scope (~$800-1200)
- Future phases (posts page, events, social media integration, expanded cemetery page, 15-20 PDF uploads) can be proposed after the basic site launches
